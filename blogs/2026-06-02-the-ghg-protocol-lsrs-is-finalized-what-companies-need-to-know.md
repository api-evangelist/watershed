The GHG Protocol has completed its Land Sector and Removals Standard (LSRS), establishing the first comprehensive framework for measuring forest, land, and agriculture emissions. Key updates include a January 1, 2027 effective date, mandatory linear discounting for land use change emissions, and more flexible supply chain traceability models like mass-balance. Companies should begin planning immediately, as calculation guidance is expected mid-2026.
